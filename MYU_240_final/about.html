Our Mission

Manhattan Youth United (MYU) provides Recreational, Academy and Select soccer leagues for boys and girls in Manhattan. The Recreation program fields teams from 4U through 19U. The Academy soccer program offers enhanced, developmental training for 8U, 9U, 10U, 11U and 12U players. The MYU soccer program offers competitive (Athena, and Classic) and the US Youth Soccer National League fields teams from 13U through 19U. Manhattan Youth United is a non-profit organization dedicated to the task of promoting and enhancing the culture of soccer in New York City. The organization shall provide the opportunity and training for the youth of New York, to learn the game of soccer through the development of individual skill, fitness, teamwork, and fair play. Each year Manhattan Youth United provides over $130,000 to over 100 scholarship players affording them an opportunity to play this beautiful game.
