Macro Photography - Hack

Step Three

  • With your camera in position, get as close as you can to the subject in manual mode until your subject comes in to focus.
  • Once you have your selected area tack sharp, slowly press down on the shutter.
  • You can achieve the desired results if you have a steady hand, but it is recommended that you use a tripod if you have access to one.
