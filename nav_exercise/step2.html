Step Two

  • Remove the lense of your choice making sure to keep the sensor free from dust and debris.
  • If you have a reverse lens clip you can screw that on now.
  • Position the lens in reverse against the rim of the camera and try to lock it in postion as best as you can if not using a lens clip.
  • Hold the lens as steady as possible and prepare for the final step.
