Which face will she wear ?

Jena DiMarzzio is known for reinventing herself throughout her career and for her versatility in numerous areas of the entertainment industry. The multi talented artist began performing as a teenager, singing at open mic nights and acting in school plays. She studied at Youth is Yours Academy, through New York University's Bloomdale School of the Arts, before dropping out to pursue a music career.
